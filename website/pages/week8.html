Goals

Both the sixth and eighth graders are putting finishing touches on their previous projects. The 6th graders touch up their earrings while the 8th graders get to show off the cardboard games they built to their classmates.

In week eight, we will be returning to using our Arduinos. This time around, we will be using them to control motors that spin a mechanical flipbook. Students will also get to create their own animation for their flipbooks.
