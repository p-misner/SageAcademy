Catapults

Question

How does changing the height of our crossbar effect how far our catapult can launch something? Do other factors influence how far a catapult is able to throw a penny?

Materials

  • Many rubber bands
  • 10 Popsicle Sticks
  • 1 Plastic Spoon
  • 1 Plastic Fork

How to Build the Catapult
Assemble Materials

Get all your materials and place them in front of you like the picture below.

Assemble Crossbar

Choose anywhere from 5-10 popsicle sticks to use as your crossbar. Wrap a rubber band around each end of the bundle of popsicle sticks to bind them together.

Build Launcher

Grab the plastic fork and plastic spoon. Place the plastic spoon on top of the fork and bind the ends togethers by wrapping a rubber band around it multiple times.

Put It All Together

Place the crossbar between the spoon and fork launcher and then use a rubber band in an X shape to put everthing together. You are ready to go!

The Catapult Game

Priya will hand out the scoreboard and cups. The goal of the game is to get a penny into the cups. Different cups have different point values. Two people compete against each other with their catapults. The rules are as follows:

  1. You get two launches per turn to get a penny into a cup.
  2. The game has three rounds to get the most points
  3. Two pennies in the same cup means double the points.
  4. If the game is tied after three rounds, each person gets one launch to break the tie.

Sending STL Files to Print

Want to print something fun for yourself for the 3D printer but can't remember the steps on how to export your file? Follow the steps below.

Step 1

Go to Tinkercad and enter your first name all lowercase as your username

Step 2

Once you join, select '3D Designs' from the menu on the right and then click the box containig the design you want to edit or the button titled 'Create new design'

Step 3

When you are done modeling, click the object you want to 3D print then click the export button, shown with the blue arrow.

Step 4

After clicking the export button, the screen in the image below pops up. Click on the STL option circled in blue. This downloads the file you need to send to Priya.

Step 5

Either email the downloaded file to Priya or submit it on schoology through the 'Personal 3D Printed Objects' assignment
