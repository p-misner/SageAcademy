Rubber Band Rockets

Question

By changing the fin shape of our rubber hand powered cup rockets, can we maximixe the amount of cups the rocket knocks over. Additionally, do certain rocket fin designs make the rocket go farther when launched?

Materials

  • Ten small dixie cups
  • Two rubber bands
  • One popsicle stick, cut in fourths with slots
  • Two large plastic cups - one rocket, one launcher
  • Cardboard, paper, tape and glue

Round 1

Using just the cup, figure out how to launch the rocket. How many cups can you knock over? In your table, fill out the amount of cups knocked over.

Round 2

Attach a nose to the rocket. Can everyone think of a different design? How many cups can you knock over? In your table, fill out the design change and the amount of cups knocked over.

Round 3

Attach fins to the rocket. Can everyone think of a different design? How many cups can you knock over? In your table, fill out the design change and the amount of cups knocked over.

Round 4

Everyone can do whatever design they want based on what they learned. How many cups can you knock over? In your table, fill out the design change and the amount of cups knocked over.

Designing Earrings
Design Limitations?

When we are designing our own earrings, what features must all earrings have? Is there a size limit? Weight limit? What about the material of these earrings

Design Limitations?

On a piece of paper, draw five earring ideas you have. After brainstorming, choose one earring idea to focus on and make a drawing of your earring the same size as it would be in real life.

Ready to start modeling?

Open your computer backup and go to Tinkercad. Create a new design and start modeling your earring!
