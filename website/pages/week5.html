Rubber Band Rockets

Question

By changing the fin shape of our rubber hand powered cup rockets, can we maximixe how high the rocket can fly. Do certain rocket fin designs or rocket nose shapes make the rocket go farther when launched?

Materials

  • Two rubber bands
  • One popsicle stick, cut in fourths with slots
  • Two large plastic cups - one rocket, one launcher
  • Cardboard, paper, tape and glue

Round 1

Using just the cup, figure out how to launch the rocket. How high did it go in inches? In your table, fill out the design change and height

Round 2

Attach a nose to the rocket. Can everyone think of a different design? How high did it go in inches? In your table, fill out the design change and height.

Round 3

Attach fins to the rocket. Can everyone think of a different design? How high did it go in inches? In your table, fill out the design change and height

Round 4

Everyone can do whatever design they want based on what they learned. How high did it go in inches? In your table, fill out the design change and height

Designing Earrings
Design Limitations?

When we are designing our own earrings, what features must all earrings have? Is there a size limit? Weight limit? What about the material of these earrings

Design Limitations?

On a piece of paper, draw five earring ideas you have. After brainstorming, choose one earring idea to focus on and make a drawing of your earring the same size as it would be in real life.

Ready to start modeling?

Open your computer backup and go to Tinkercad. Create a new design and start modeling your earring!
